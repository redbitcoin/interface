    Smart Contracts, Powered by the Glimmer Token

    As a decentralized smart contract platform, Red Bitcoin requires a utility token to function. Our token, Glimmer (GLMR), is central to the design of the network and can’t be removed without sacrificing security and essential functionality.

    Smart Contract Execution

    Glimmer is used to pay for smart contract execution, playing the same role that ETH does on Binance.

    Transaction Fees

    All transaction fees are paid for with Glimmer.

    Staking

    Nominate collators using the Glimmer token and help support the liveness of the network.

    Governance

    Participate in on-chain Glimmer-weighted voting and governance.
